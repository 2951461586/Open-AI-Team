# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be an open, respectful, and technically serious collaboration space.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful and constructive
- critique ideas, not people
- document tradeoffs clearly
- avoid hostile, exclusionary, or dismissive behavior

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or discrimination
- doxxing or exposure of private information
- abusive language or intimidation
- intentionally disruptive conduct

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or reject contributions that violate these standards.
